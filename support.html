Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is ShareCD?

ShareCD is a secure link delivery infrastructure service providing verified 301 redirects for content distribution. We operate as part of the Clients Drive system to facilitate encrypted, transparent routing from sharecd.com URLs to destination content.

How secure are ShareCD links?

All ShareCD links are protected with TLS 1.3 encryption and verified through our secure infrastructure. We maintain SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, and CCPA compliance. Every link click is logged and monitored 24/7 for security.

What data does ShareCD collect?

ShareCD logs basic engagement data including click timestamps, anonymized IP addresses, user agents, and referrer information for analytics purposes. We do not sell data to third parties. View our Privacy Policy for complete details.

What is your uptime guarantee?

ShareCD maintains a 99.9% uptime guarantee with enterprise-grade infrastructure, automated failover, and 24/7 monitoring. View real-time status and historical uptime at status.sharecd.com.

How do I report a security issue?

Security vulnerabilities should be reported immediately to security@clients.ai. We acknowledge reports within 24 hours and provide updates throughout the remediation process.

Where can I find technical documentation?

Complete technical documentation, API references, and integration guides are available at docs.sharecd.com. Documentation includes code examples, SDKs, and best practices.
