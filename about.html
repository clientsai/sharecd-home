What We Do

Secure Link Infrastructure

ShareCD is a neutral utility service providing secure 301 redirects for content delivery. Operating as part of the Clients Drive system, we facilitate verified link routing with full transparency.

Every sharecd.com link is encrypted, verified, and logged—bridging senders and recipients with enterprise-grade reliability.

How ShareCD Works

1

Link Creation

Sender creates a sharecd.com link pointing to destination content

2

Verification & Routing

ShareCD verifies the link, logs engagement, and performs secure 301 redirect

3

Delivery & Analytics

Recipient arrives at destination with full transparency; analytics shared with sender
