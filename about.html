About ShareCD

Secure link delivery infrastructure

Overview

ShareCD is a neutral utility service providing secure 301 redirects for content delivery. Operating as part of the Clients Drive system, ShareCD facilitates verified link routing from sharecd.com URLs to destination content with full transparency and security.

Our Mission

To provide enterprise-grade link delivery infrastructure that prioritizes security, transparency, and reliability. ShareCD bridges the gap between senders and recipients with verified, encrypted routing.

How ShareCD Works

ShareCD operates as a lightweight redirect service with the following workflow:

  • Sender creates a sharecd.com link pointing to destination content
  • Recipient clicks the ShareCD link
  • ShareCD verifies the link, logs engagement data, and performs a secure 301 redirect
  • Engagement data is shared with the sender and partner network for analytics
  • Recipient arrives at destination content with full transparency

Infrastructure

ShareCD is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with 99.9% uptime guarantee. All traffic is encrypted end-to-end with TLS 1.3 and 256-bit AES encryption. Our systems are monitored 24/7 with automated failover and redundancy.

Compliance & Certifications

ShareCD maintains the following security and compliance standards:

  • SOC 2 Type II — Annual security audits and controls
  • ISO 27001 — Information security management certification
  • GDPR Compliant — European data protection standards
  • CCPA Compliant — California privacy rights
